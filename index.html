About Me

I'm currently a Senior Research Scientist at Sony Interactive Entertainment (PlayStation), where I work on AI-enhanced gaming content and video quality assessment. My work bridges academia and industry, and I’ve contributed to over 30+ peer-reviewed publications, 10 patents, and several open-source QoE datasets.

Previously, I held roles at Brightcove Inc., Kingston University, Bell Labs, and QoE-NET under the Horizon 2020 program. I have a strong foundation in multimedia communication, video streaming, deep learning, and computational visual quality modeling. I'm an active contributor to IEEE standards and conferences, and currently serve as the Chair of the VQEG Emerging Technologies Group.
